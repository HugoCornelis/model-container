Notes:

DB interface fetches morphology from the neuromorpho DB, over the internet.

Modelcontainer stores the morphology / model, and makes it available for other software components.

The Heccer integrator fetches the morphology / model from the model container, and builds an intermediary representation suitable for simulation.

Outputs are constructed from the SSP scheduler configuration.

The Heccer solver and output objects are activated and perform the simulation.

Direct memory access for communication -> efficient

Note: data in the run-time environment is layed on an axis ranging from biological concepts to numerics. I will come back to this property a couple of times during the presentation.
