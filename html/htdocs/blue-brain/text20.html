Hierarchy level 2

Specifies, on purpose, to many software components, their behavior, and especially their interactions.
With 'to many' we mean that it is ok that when a physical implementation merges two or three components, although we believe that it will constrain the usefulness of the implementation, as it does in the case of Genesis 2.

There is a special mention of a Genesis 2 compatibility layer, for community reasons. In principle this is a part of the 'core bridge', which translates conceptual states of a CBI simulator between different simulation paradigms.
